ABOUT ME

I'm Jacob "Yacklebeam" Troxel, a developer, gamer, Twitch streamer, and D&D enthusiast from Spokane, WA, and my passion is developing video games.


In May of 2015, I graduated from Gonzaga University with a degree in Computer Science, and now spend my free time attempting to bring my ideas to life as games. My specialization is C++ and Java development, but I'm competent programming in Javascript and know my way around various web technologies. I'm extremely interested in low-level game engine design, something I try to expirement with often.


When I'm not developing games, I'm playing them (usually live on Twitch.tv). You can find there most mornings playing RPGs and Adventure games.
